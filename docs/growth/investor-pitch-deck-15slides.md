## Slide 4 — The Solution: Justice OS + CoTrackPro

### Two Products, One Mission

**Justice OS** — Open-source infrastructure layer
- Authentication, billing, AI, document generation, audit trails
- 72-project ecosystem of legal tech modules
- Deploy in minutes; extend infinitely

**CoTrackPro** — SaaS case tracking platform built on Justice OS
- Tracks cases, deadlines, documents, and hearings
- Multi-jurisdiction support (50 states + federal)
- Role-based access: attorneys, advocates, self-represented litigants
- AI-powered document drafting and deadline calculation

**Why both?**
- Justice OS drives adoption and developer network effects
- CoTrackPro is the monetization layer and proof-of-concept
- Together they create a flywheel: more developers → better platform → more customers
