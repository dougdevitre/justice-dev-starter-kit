# Partnership Strategy — Justice OS

> **8-Page Comprehensive Strategic Partnership Plan**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Justice OS and CoTrackPro are building the open infrastructure layer for equitable legal technology. Our partnership strategy is designed to accelerate adoption, amplify impact, and create a sustainable ecosystem of collaborators across the legal technology landscape.

**Partnership Vision:** By partnering with legal tech platforms, court systems, legal aid organizations, universities, and government agencies, Justice OS will become the default infrastructure for access-to-justice technology within 36 months.

**Strategic Goals (12-Month):**
- 5–10 formal partnership agreements signed
- 3+ court system pilots or deployments
- 10+ legal aid organizations on platform
- 5+ law school curriculum integrations
- 2+ government innovation contracts

**Why Partnerships Are Core to Our Strategy:**
Justice OS is not a company that can grow through traditional B2B sales alone. Trust and credibility in the legal ecosystem are built through institutional relationships. Partnerships compress our go-to-market timeline from years to months.

### 2.2 Court Systems (Deployment Partnerships)

**Objective:** Deploy CoTrackPro and Justice OS modules directly within court infrastructure to enable e-filing, case management, and self-represented litigant support.

**Target Jurisdictions:**

| State | Court Level | Opportunity |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| California | Superior Court | Self-help center technology |
| Texas | District Court | Remote hearing management |
| New York | Family Court | Case tracking for litigants |
| Florida | Circuit Court | Document generation portal |
| Illinois | Circuit Court | Pro se litigant tools |
| Federal (US) | District Courts | Pilot program via SJI grant |

**Approach:**
1. Begin with court self-help centers (less procurement friction)
2. Pilot → evaluate → expand to full docket management
3. Partner with State Justice Institute (SJI) for funding
4. Align with NCSC (National Center for State Courts) for credibility

**Value to Courts:**
- Reduce clerk workload by automating document intake
- Improve case resolution rates for self-represented litigants
- Lower cost-per-case vs. custom software
