# Media & PR Strategy — Justice OS (Quarterly)

> **Rolling PR Strategy + Media List for Year 1**

---

## PR Strategy Overview

**Goals:**
- Establish Justice OS as the leading voice in access-to-justice technology
- Generate 50+ earned media mentions in Year 1
- Build relationships with 20+ journalists on the legal tech beat
- Support sales pipeline through thought leadership and credibility

**PR Philosophy:**
> We don't chase press. We create stories so compelling that journalists come to us.

**Key Messages:**
1. Legal tech has failed the people who need it most — until now
2. Justice OS is the open infrastructure layer for equitable legal technology
3. Every feature we ship is in service of people who can't afford a lawyer
4. The 72-project ecosystem is how we ensure no one gets left behind

---

## Quarterly Focus Areas

### Q1: Product Launch
**Theme:** CoTrackPro is here — and it's built for legal aid, not law firms

**Key Stories:**
- Product launch announcement
- Founding story (why we built this)
- First customer stories
- Open-source ecosystem announcement

**Target Outcomes:**
- 10+ articles about product launch
- 3 podcast interviews (legal tech focus)
- 5 social media amplifications by influencers
- 2+ speaking slots confirmed

### Q2: Thought Leadership
**Theme:** The future of legal technology must be equitable

**Key Stories:**
- State of access to justice (data-driven report)
- "What legal tech gets wrong" opinion piece
- Community-driven development model
- AI and the justice gap

**Target Outcomes:**
- 2 bylined articles in major legal publications
- 1 major conference keynote or panel
- 5+ journalist relationships cultivated
- 1 research collaboration announced

### Q3: Impact
**Theme:** Real people, real outcomes — what justice technology looks like in practice

**Key Stories:**
- Customer case studies (2–3 detailed stories)
- Impact metrics release (cases managed, docs generated)
- Community contributor spotlights
- Partnership announcements

**Target Outcomes:**
- 3 human-interest stories placed
- 10+ articles referencing our impact data
- Partnership press releases (2–3)
- Video case studies published

### Q4: Funding/Partnerships
**Theme:** Justice OS is scaling — and we're looking for partners who believe in this mission

**Key Stories:**
- Seed round announcement
- Partnership announcements
- Year-in-review (metrics + stories)
- Vision for Year 2

**Target Outcomes:**
- Funding announcement in TechCrunch, VentureBeat, or equivalent
- 5+ tier-1 partnership announcements
- Year-end retrospective placed in 3+ publications
- Speaking slots for next year confirmed

## Outreach Strategy

### Press Releases (Quarterly)

**Template — Product Launch:**
```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Justice OS Launches CoTrackPro: Free Case Management for Legal Aid Organizations

[City, Date] — Justice OS, the open-source legal technology company building
infrastructure for access to justice, today announced the launch of CoTrackPro,
a free and low-cost case management platform designed for legal aid organizations
and courts serving self-represented litigants.

[1–2 sentences on the problem CoTrackPro solves]

"Quote from founder about mission and impact."

[Key features and availability]

[Metrics / traction]

[Boilerplate about Justice OS]

Contact: press@justiceos.dev
```

**Distribution:**
- PR Newswire or Business Wire (for major announcements)
- Direct email to top 30 reporters on the beat
- Social media announcement
- Investor + advisor notification

### Media Pitches (Monthly)

**Format:** 200-word personalized email to individual reporter

**Template:**
```
Subject: [Specific angle tied to their recent coverage]

Hi [Reporter],

I saw your recent piece on [specific article]. I'm working on a story that I
think your readers would care about.

[1 sentence: what is happening]
[1 sentence: why it matters to their audience]
[1 sentence: what makes it different from existing coverage]

I have access to:
- [Exclusive data or insight]
- [Customer story]
- [Expert source]

Would you be interested in an exclusive? I can get you everything you need by
[date].

[Founder]
```

### Thought Leadership Submissions (Ongoing)

**Target Publications for Bylines:**
1. Law Technology Today (ABA) — "The Open Source Future of Legal Aid Tech"
2. Harvard Law Review Blog — "Access to Justice Infrastructure: A New Model"
3. Fast Company — "Why Legal Tech Has Failed Everyone Except Law Firms"
4. Stanford Social Innovation Review — "The Case for Open Legal Infrastructure"

**Submission Guidelines:**
- 800–1,500 words
- First-person, opinionated, data-backed
- No product pitches — pure thought leadership
- Include credibility (founder title, mission of org)

### Relationship Building (Continuous)

**Monthly Rituals:**
- Follow and engage with 5 new journalists per week on Twitter/LinkedIn
- Respond to journalist requests (HARO, #journorequest)
- Send 2–3 genuine "saw your story + thought you'd find this useful" emails
- Invite 1 journalist per month to demo or background briefing

**Annual Media Relationships Goal:** 25+ journalists who know your name and story.
