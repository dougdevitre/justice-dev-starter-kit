## CRM Configuration

### Pipedrive / HubSpot Setup

**Pipeline Stages:**
1. Identified — Added to CRM; not yet contacted
2. Researched — Background research complete; contact found
3. Outreach Sent — First email/message sent
4. Replied — They responded (positive or neutral)
5. Meeting Scheduled — Call/demo booked
6. Proposal Sent — Formal proposal or MOU sent
7. Negotiating — Actively discussing terms
8. Closed Won — Partnership signed
9. Closed Lost — No longer pursuing (with reason)
10. Nurture — Not now, but revisit in 90 days

**Custom Fields per Prospect:**
- Partner Category (see below)
- Tier (startup / growth / enterprise)
- Annual Revenue / Budget
- Key Decision Maker
- Technical Contact
- Primary Value Prop
- Last Contact Date
- Next Follow-Up Date
- Source (referral / conference / inbound / outbound)
