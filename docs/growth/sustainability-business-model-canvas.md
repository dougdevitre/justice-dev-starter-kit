# Sustainability & Business Model Canvas — Justice OS

> **Lean Canvas + Financial Projections for Long-Term Organizational Sustainability**

---

## 1. Business Model Canvas

| Block | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Problem** | 80% of people who need legal help can't afford it; legal tech is built for law firms, not people |
| **Customer Segments** | Legal aid orgs, court self-help centers, law schools, self-represented litigants, government agencies |
| **Unique Value Proposition** | Open-source legal infrastructure that deploys in hours, not months — built for access to justice |
| **Solution** | Justice OS (open-source modules) + CoTrackPro (SaaS case management) |
| **Channels** | Direct outbound, partner referrals, conference circuit, content/SEO, developer community |
| **Revenue Streams** | SaaS subscriptions, managed hosting, professional services, government contracts, licensing |
| **Cost Structure** | Engineering (50%), sales & marketing (25%), infrastructure (10%), operations (15%) |
| **Key Metrics** | MRR, CAC, LTV, churn rate, contributor count, module adoption |
| **Unfair Advantage** | 72-project ecosystem, community network effects, open-source credibility, mission alignment |

## 5. Breakeven Analysis

**Fixed Monthly Costs (Year 1):** $130,000/mo

**Variable Costs per Customer:**
- Infrastructure: $50–$200/mo (depending on usage)
- Support: ~2 hrs × $50/hr = $100/mo
- Total variable: ~$150–$300/mo per enterprise customer

**Breakeven Calculation:**

| Scenario | Customers Needed | ARR at Breakeven |
|----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Enterprise only ($2,000 ACV/mo) | 72 customers | $1.73M ARR |
| Mixed (50% enterprise, 50% nonprofit) | 95 customers | $1.82M ARR |
| With gov contracts ($200K/yr) | 60 enterprise + 1 gov | $1.64M ARR |

**Estimated Breakeven:** Month 18–24 (assuming $2M seed funding and 18% MoM growth)
