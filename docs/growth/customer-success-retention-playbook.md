# Customer Success & Retention Playbook — Justice OS

> **Post-Sale Execution Playbook for Enterprise Customers and Community Contributors**

---

## Overview

Customer success at Justice OS is not an afterthought — it is a core competitive advantage. Our customers choose us because of mission alignment; they stay because of exceptional outcomes.

**Customer Success Philosophy:**
> "We succeed when our customers succeed. Our job is not to support their use of our software — it is to ensure they achieve the outcomes they came here for."

**Key Principles:**
- Proactive, not reactive — we reach out before problems arise
- Outcome-focused — every interaction tied to customer goals
- Scalable — playbooks that work across all customer types
- Human — we treat customers as people, not accounts

---

## Part 1: Enterprise Customer Playbook

### Pre-Sale Handoff (Sales → CSM)

When a deal closes, the Account Executive (AE) sends a handoff document to the Customer Success Manager (CSM) within 24 hours:

**Handoff Document Template:**
```
Customer: ___________
Contract Value: $___  Term: ___ months  Renewal: ___
Key Contact(s): ___________
Executive Sponsor: ___________

WHY THEY BOUGHT
- Primary pain point: ___________
- Success metric they care most about: ___________
- Timeline expectations: ___________

RISKS / RED FLAGS
- ___________

PROMISES MADE
- ___________

CHAMPION NOTES
- What motivates the champion: ___________
- Internal politics to be aware of: ___________
```

### Week 1–4: Onboarding

**Goals:** Customer is up and running, team is trained, first value is delivered

**Week 1: Technical Setup**

| Day | Task | Owner |
|-----|------|-------|
| Day 1 | Welcome email + calendar invite for kickoff | CSM |
| Day 1 | Kickoff call (45 min) — goals, timeline, contacts | CSM + customer |
| Day 2 | Account provisioned; admin users added | Engineering |
| Day 3 | Technical checklist sent; integration guide shared | CSM |
| Day 5 | Technical setup call (30 min) — verify configuration | CSM + IT contact |

**Kickoff Call Agenda:**
1. Introductions (5 min)
2. Review goals and success metrics (10 min)
3. Review onboarding timeline (5 min)
4. Technical setup overview (10 min)
5. Establish communication cadence (5 min)
6. Open Q&A (10 min)

**Week 2: Data Migration & Configuration**

| Day | Task | Owner |
|-----|------|-------|
| Day 8 | Data migration plan finalized | CSM + IT |
| Day 10 | Test data migration completed | Engineering |
| Day 11 | Customer reviews test data | Customer |
| Day 12 | Configuration customized to customer workflows | CSM |
| Day 14 | End of week check-in (15 min) | CSM |

**Week 3: Training**

| Day | Task | Owner |
|-----|------|-------|
| Day 15 | Admin training session (60 min) | CSM |
| Day 17 | End-user training session (60 min) | CSM |
| Day 18 | Training materials shared (video + docs) | CSM |
| Day 19 | First real cases entered by customer team | Customer |
| Day 21 | First value check-in: "How did the first week go?" | CSM |

**Week 4: Go-Live**

| Day | Task | Owner |
|-----|------|-------|
| Day 22 | Full system go-live | Customer |
| Day 24 | 48-hour post-go-live check (5 min call) | CSM |
| Day 28 | 30-day onboarding review (30 min) | CSM + exec sponsor |

**30-Day Onboarding Review Agenda:**
1. Metrics: How many cases created? Documents generated?
2. Adoption: Which features are being used? Which aren't?
3. Feedback: What's working? What needs improvement?
4. Next 30 days: What does success look like?

### Week 5–8: Training & Enablement

**Goals:** Increase feature adoption from 30% → 60%; establish self-service habits

**Training Program:**

| Session | Format | Duration | Content |
|---------|--------|---------|---------|
| Power User Workshop | Video call | 90 min | Advanced features, shortcuts, automation |
| Document Generation Deep Dive | Webinar | 60 min | Creating custom templates |
| Reporting & Analytics | 1:1 or group | 60 min | Pulling reports; measuring outcomes |
| Integrations Workshop | 1:1 | 45 min | API setup, third-party connections |

**Enablement Assets:**
- Knowledge base (docs.justiceos.dev)
- Video tutorial library (10+ videos)
- Workflow templates for common case types
- Email templates for client communication
- Checklist: "Are you getting full value from CoTrackPro?"

### Ongoing: Adoption Tracking (Weekly)

**Weekly Usage Metrics to Monitor:**

| Metric | Healthy | At Risk | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Weekly active users / seats | > 70% | 40–70% | < 40% |
| Cases created (last 7 days) | Growing | Flat | Declining |
| Documents generated | Used weekly | Rarely | Not used |
| Last login (days ago) | < 3 days | 3–14 days | > 14 days |
| Support tickets open | 0–1 | 2–3 | > 3 |

**Automated Health Score Alert Rules:**
- If usage drops > 20% week-over-week: CSM notified automatically
- If no logins in 7 days: CSM sends check-in email
- If health score < 40: Escalate to VP Customer Success

**Weekly Check-In Email Template (6–12 weeks):**
```
Subject: Your weekly CoTrackPro update

Hi [Name],

Quick update from your account this week:
- Cases in progress: [X]
- Documents generated: [X]
- Team activity: [High / Normal / Low]

[If low activity]: I noticed activity was lower than usual this week — is
there anything blocking your team? Happy to jump on a quick call.

[If normal/high]: Great to see the team using the platform. 

Talk soon,
[CSM Name]
```

### Monthly: Health Checks

**Monthly Business Review (MBR) — 30 min call**

**Agenda:**
1. Metrics review: Cases, docs, users, outcomes (10 min)
2. Wins from last month (5 min)
3. Challenges + support tickets (5 min)
4. Feature updates + roadmap preview (5 min)
5. Upcoming renewals or expansions (5 min)

**MBR Health Score Review:**
Rate each dimension 1–5:
- Product adoption
- Outcome achievement
- Team satisfaction
- Support experience
- Executive sponsor engagement

Overall health: Green (4–5) / Yellow (3) / Red (1–2)

---

### Quarterly: Expansion Opportunities

**Quarterly Business Review (QBR) — 60 min**

**Participants:** CSM + AE + executive sponsor + customer champion

**Agenda:**
1. Quarter in review: Metrics + outcomes (15 min)
2. ROI review: Time saved, outcomes, testimonials (10 min)
3. Roadmap preview + upcoming features (10 min)
4. Expansion discussion: More users? New use cases? New teams? (15 min)
5. Renewal planning (10 min)

**Expansion Playbook:**
- User expansion: If using > 80% of seats, propose upsell
- Feature expansion: If not using AI or integrations, run enablement campaign
- Team expansion: If one department is using it, propose rollout to others
- Case type expansion: If using for housing, propose adding family or immigration

**Expansion Revenue Target:** 120% NRR (net revenue retention)

### Retention Metrics

| Metric | Target | Review Cadence |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Gross Revenue Retention | > 90% | Monthly |
| Net Revenue Retention (NRR) | > 115% | Monthly |
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | > 4.2/5 | Per interaction |
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | > 50 | Quarterly |
| Churn Rate | < 2%/mo | Monthly |
| Time to First Value | < 14 days | Per customer |
| Onboarding Completion Rate | > 90% | Monthly |

**Churn Prevention Playbook:**
1. Risk identified (health score < 40 or churn signal)
2. CSM reaches out within 24 hours
3. Root cause identified on call
4. Remediation plan proposed within 48 hours
5. Escalate to executive if needed within 72 hours
6. Weekly monitoring until health restored

## Part 2: Community Contributor Playbook

### Contributor Recognition (Monthly)

**Monthly Contributor Spotlight:**
- Identify top contributor by pull requests merged, issues resolved, or community engagement
- Write 300-word spotlight post for newsletter + website
- Share on LinkedIn and Twitter
- Send personal thank-you from founder

**Monthly Recognition Actions:**
- [ ] DM top contributor on Discord or email
- [ ] Post spotlight in #announcements channel
- [ ] Include in monthly newsletter
- [ ] Add to Contributors Hall of Fame (website)
- [ ] Send swag (if first time reaching Contributor tier)

---

### Maintainer Support (Ongoing)

**Maintainer Support Package:**
- Monthly 30-minute 1:1 with founding team member
- Access to private #maintainers-lounge on Discord
- Early access to roadmap and feature discussions
- Travel grant ($500–$2,000/yr) for conference talks
- Annual Justice OS Summit invitation (travel covered)

**Maintainer Monthly Check-In Template:**
```
Hey [Name],

Quick monthly check-in for [Module Name]:

1. How are things going with the module? Anything blocking you?
2. Any PRs or issues that need attention or discussion?
3. Anything you need from the core team?
4. Any contributors you'd like to spotlight or elevate?

Thanks for everything you're doing — it matters.

[Founder]
```

### Project Health Tracking (Weekly)

**Module Health Dashboard:**

| Metric | Healthy | Needs Attention | Critical |
|--------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| Last commit | < 7 days | 7–30 days | > 30 days |
| Open PRs | 0–3 | 4–8 | > 8 |
| Open issues | 0–10 | 11–25 | > 25 |
| CI passing | Yes | Flaky | Failing |
| Active maintainer | Yes | Inactive | None |
| Documentation | Complete | Partial | Missing |

**Weekly Module Review Process:**
1. Run automated GitHub report every Monday
2. Flag any modules in "Needs Attention" or "Critical" status
3. CSM or community manager reaches out to maintainer within 24 hours
4. Core team provides support if maintainer unavailable
