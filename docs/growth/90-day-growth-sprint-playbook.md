# 90-Day Growth Sprint Playbook — Justice OS

> **Week-by-Week Tactical Execution Plan for Explosive Growth**

---

## Sprint Overview

**Goal:** In 90 days, generate $50K+ in pipeline, onboard first paying customers, establish community presence, and position for seed funding.

**Themes:**
- Weeks 1–2: Foundation (set up everything)
- Weeks 3–4: Outreach (start conversations)
- Weeks 5–6: Engagement (respond + refine)
- Weeks 7–8: Conversion (close deals)
- Weeks 9–10: Scale (amplify what's working)
- Weeks 11–12: Harvest (analyze, celebrate, plan)

**Daily Rhythm:**
- 8:00–8:15 AM: Daily standup (solo or team)
- 8:15–10:00 AM: Deep work (product or strategy)
- 10:00 AM–12:00 PM: Outreach and partnerships
- 1:00–3:00 PM: Customer calls, demos, meetings
- 3:00–4:00 PM: Admin, email, follow-ups
- 4:00–4:30 PM: Daily log + tomorrow's plan

- [ ] Pull full 90-day analytics report (all 7 dashboards)

- [ ] Team celebration for first paying customers
- [ ] Thank-you emails to everyone who helped (advisors, partners)
- [ ] Post "90-day update" on LinkedIn (authentic + specific)
- [ ] Send investor update (if applicable)
- [ ] Community shoutout in monthly newsletter

**Plan Next Quarter:**
- [ ] Set Q2 OKRs (based on Q1 learnings)
- [ ] Identify 3 highest-leverage activities to double down on
- [ ] Determine if hiring is needed (see scaling-checklist.md)
- [ ] Plan next fundraising milestone

**Scale What Works:**
- [ ] Automate top-performing email sequences

- [ ] Create more content on highest-traffic topics
- [ ] Expand to second target market (e.g., courts if started with legal aid)

**Deliverables by Day 90:**
- Full analytics report complete
- Q2 OKRs set and committed to

- $100K+ ARR on trajectory
