## Appendix — Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We don't have budget" | We offer grant-billing support and can provide a letter for your next grant cycle |
| "Our data can't leave our servers" | We offer on-prem and hybrid deployment — full data residency control |
| "We already have a system" | CoTrackPro integrates via API; we complement existing tools rather than replace them |
| "We need HIPAA compliance" | CoTrackPro is designed HIPAA-first; we can walk through our BAA process |
| "It's too complex for our staff" | Role-specific training and onboarding is included; each user sees only what they need |
| "We need government procurement" | We support cooperative purchasing contracts and can join your approved vendor list |
