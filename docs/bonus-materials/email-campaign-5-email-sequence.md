## Email 2 — Compliance Deep Dive

**Subject:** "What about our data security?" — Here's the answer.

**Send timing:** Day 4–5  
**Read time:** 3–4 minutes

---

Hi [First Name],

When we talk to legal aid organizations, courts, and government agencies, security and compliance are always the first questions. They should be.

You're dealing with some of the most sensitive data imaginable — custody records, domestic violence disclosures, children's educational records, immigration status. Getting this wrong isn't just a liability. It causes real harm to real people.

Here's how CoTrackPro handles it:

**HIPAA Compliance**
- Protected health information (PHI) is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA) available for covered entities
- Access controls restrict PHI to authorized roles only
- Every access event is logged in a tamper-resistant audit trail

**FERPA Compliance**
- Student and family education records handled per FERPA requirements
- Consent management built into the data model
- Disclosure logging and reporting included

**Infrastructure Security**
- Hosted on AWS (SOC 2 Type II eligible infrastructure)
- Role-based access control enforced at the data layer — not just the UI
- Multi-factor authentication supported
- Rate limiting and DDoS protection at the edge

**What this means for you:**
Your data doesn't become our liability problem. We designed CoTrackPro so that your IT team, General Counsel, and compliance officers can say yes.

## Campaign Notes

### Personalization Checklist
Before sending each email, customize:
- [ ] `[First Name]` → actual first name
- [ ] `[Prospect Organization]` → their org name
- [ ] `[Similar Org Type]` in Email 3 → match their org type
- [ ] `[state/region]` → their geography if known
- [ ] Subject lines → A/B test 2–3 variants per email

### Tracking Metrics
- Open rate (target: 30%+)
- Click-through rate (target: 5%+)
- Reply rate (target: 5%+)
- Demo bookings per 100 sends (target: 2+)

### Recommended Tools
- Email sequencing: Apollo.io, Instantly, or Mailshake
- CRM tracking: HubSpot (free tier) or Airtable
- Booking: Calendly (free tier)

### Follow-Up After Demo
If they book a demo → send the security overview doc within 24 hours of the call.  
If they go quiet after the demo → send a 1-sentence check-in 7 days later.
